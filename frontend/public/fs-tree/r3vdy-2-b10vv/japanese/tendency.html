Tendency, Degree & State

Suffixes and patterns that describe tendencies, degrees, states of being, and how much of a quality something has.

Tendency — "tend to"

Pattern Attaches to Meaning Example
～がち ます stem / noun + がち Tend to (negative tendency) — happens too often. 遅れがち（おくれがち）— Tends to be late.
病気がち（びょうきがち）— Prone to illness.
～っぽい ます stem / noun / い-adj stem + っぽい -ish / -like — gives the impression of. Often slightly negative. 忘れっぽい（わすれっぽい）— Forgetful.
子供っぽい（こどもっぽい）— Childish.
白っぽい（しろっぽい）— Whitish.
～気味（ぎみ） ます stem / noun + 気味 Slightly / a touch of — beginning of an undesirable state. 風邪気味（かぜぎみ）— Slight cold.
太り気味（ふとりぎみ）— Slightly overweight.

Completion & Extent — "fully" / "too much"

Pattern Attaches to Meaning Example
～きる / ～きれる ます stem + きる Completely / to the end — do something fully. 走りきった（はしりきった）— Ran all the way.
食べきれない（たべきれない）— Can't eat it all.
～きれない ます stem + きれない Cannot finish / too much to fully do. 数えきれない（かぞえきれない）— Too many to count.
～すぎる ます stem / adj stem + すぎる Too much — excess. 食べすぎた（たべすぎた）— Ate too much.
高すぎる（たかすぎる）— Too expensive.
～かける / ～かけ ます stem + かける Half-done / about to start — incomplete action. 読みかけの本（よみかけのほん）— A half-read book.
言いかけてやめた（いいかけてやめた）— Started to say it but stopped.

State — "covered in" / "full of"

Pattern Attaches to Meaning Example
～だらけ Noun + だらけ Full of / covered in — always negative. 間違いだらけ（まちがいだらけ）— Full of mistakes.
ゴミだらけ — Covered in garbage.
～まみれ Noun + まみれ Covered in / smeared with — physical coating, always negative. 泥まみれ（どろまみれ）— Covered in mud.
血まみれ（ちまみれ）— Covered in blood.
～ずくめ Noun + ずくめ All / nothing but — entirely composed of. 黒ずくめ（くろずくめ）— All in black.
いいことずくめ — Nothing but good things.

だらけ vs まみれ vs ずくめ

だらけ まみれ ずくめ
Type Scattered throughout Physically coated on surface Entirely composed of
Nuance Negative (undesirable abundance) Negative (physically dirty/messy) Can be positive or negative
Example 傷だらけ（きずだらけ）— Covered in scratches 汗まみれ（あせまみれ）— Drenched in sweat 黒ずくめ — Dressed all in black

Difficulty

Pattern Attaches to Meaning Example
～やすい ます stem + やすい Easy to — tendency or ease. わかりやすい — Easy to understand.
壊れやすい（こわれやすい）— Breaks easily / fragile.
～にくい ます stem + にくい Hard to / difficult to. 読みにくい（よみにくい）— Hard to read.
食べにくい — Hard to eat.
～づらい ます stem + づらい Hard to — with psychological difficulty or discomfort. 言いづらい（いいづらい）— Hard to say (emotionally).
話しづらい（はなしづらい）— Hard to talk to.
～がたい ます stem + がたい Hard to — almost impossible, formal/literary. 信じがたい（しんじがたい）— Hard to believe.
許しがたい（ゆるしがたい）— Unforgivable.
